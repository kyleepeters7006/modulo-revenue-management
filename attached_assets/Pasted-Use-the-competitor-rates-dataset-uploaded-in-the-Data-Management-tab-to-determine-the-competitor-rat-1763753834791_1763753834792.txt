Use the competitor rates dataset uploaded in the Data Management tab to determine the competitor rate for each Location/campus we have on the rent roll. If, for example, you want to pull the competitor rate of an AL Studio - Private at Battle Creek - 501, you will look for that rent roll location in the "TrilogyCampusName" column in the competitor data file uploaded and the competitors for that location will be in the column named "CompetitorFacilityName". You would then choose the competitor with the highest weight for the service line, in this case AL. Weight columns follow the format: [ServiceLineShortName]_Comp_Weight (e.g., AL_Comp_Weight for Assisted Living).
For SL and VIL service lines, use the same weight column (they share one).
Important: Some campuses do not have any competitors with weights for a service line. In those cases, select the closest competitor that has rates based on the DrivingTime column.
